You escape Julius Caesar's prison in a daze, unsure of who you are nor why you were there. A pulsing sharp pain pounds through your head, making it hard to even think. Maybe you suffered a bad head injury while imprisoned? Or maybe while you were escaping? You can't remember, but all you know is that you need to get as far away from Caesar as possible. Through what seems like a miracle, a small memory still lives in your brain: an uncle of yours who lives in a kingdom not too far from here. The perfect place to hide.
