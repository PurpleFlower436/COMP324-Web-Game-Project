Finally, after your long travels, you finally reach your uncle's kingdom on the 5th day. As you approach the entrance, the guards eye you up and down, wary of your dishelved appearance. "State your business!" one commands. You request for a meeting with the king, claiming to be his nephew. They're reluctant to comply, skeptical of your story, but someone from your uncle's court happens to walk by, recognizing you and convincing the guards to let you in.
