Fully rested, you wake up the next morning and gather your things, prepared for another long day of travel. After traveling for a bit, you’re stopped by what sounds like an argument in the distance. Inching closer, you find a group of guards harassing a fiesty Satyr. “We know you’re part of a coup group, satyr!” A guard accused. The satyr smirked and crossed their arms. “I have no idea what you’re talking about.” They replied, their tone implying otherwise.
